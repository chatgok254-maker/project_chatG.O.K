          - it is the county governor's responsibility to create a community.
          - it is the county citizens responsibility to join communities created by the governor
          - Example workflow: Suppose member A of community M writes and sends a message. The message first goes to all members of community M who must vote for it so that the message can proceed   to the governor. If two-thirds vote for it. The message proceeds to the governor.

          - KES 50/= will be required to validate national Identification numbers with I.P.R.S.
